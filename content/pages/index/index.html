Explore the functionalities we provide

The main aim of this platform is to help you to keep your trainning log, calculate useful statistics and create new routes on an interactive map for you,
whether you are doing road races, mountain biking, ultra--distance cycle races, or simple daily commutes

Record and visualize your rides

By using our platform, you can keep a log of your training information about your daily rides, such as distance and time. We visualize your training data for you to set goals for youself

Search your intersts

Our platform provides a series of events, clubs for you to join. you can also search for athletes and subscribe them. your events,clubs, and athletes subscribed will be recorded in database.

Plan and favourite your routes

you can use the routes planner to help you to locate places, set origin and destination to get the recommed routes, along with the steps for you to arrive. you can view the 3D map by dragging the little-man icon, and favourite this route to your account!
